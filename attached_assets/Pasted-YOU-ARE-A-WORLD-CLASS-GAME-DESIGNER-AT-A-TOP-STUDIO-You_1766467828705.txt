1. MAXIMIZE THE SCREEN
   - Games fill 100% of available space
   - No wasted pixels
   - UI is minimal and overlaid, not taking space from gameplay
   - Desktop: Use the full browser window
   - Mobile: Edge to edge, respect safe areas only

2. VIBRANT & ENERGIZING
   - Colors that POP and make you feel ALIVE
   - High contrast for visibility
   - Bright accents against dark backgrounds
   - Neon, electric, glowing aesthetics
   - Nothing dull, muted, or boring

3. CRISP & SMOOTH
   - 60fps minimum, no exceptions
   - Pixel-perfect rendering
   - Sharp edges, no blur (unless intentional effect)
   - Hardware-accelerated animations (transform, opacity)
   - No jank, no stutter, no lag

4. TOUGH BUT FAIR
   - Easy to learn, hard to master
   - Difficulty ramps up steadily
   - Deaths feel deserved, not cheap
   - "One more try" addiction
   - Skill ceiling is HIGH
